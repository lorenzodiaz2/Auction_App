In this simple app you can place bids on different products.

All you have to do is go to the Auctions page, find an auction you like, copy its ID, and then go to the Bids page to place your bid.


On the Auctions page, you can also create an auction, as long as the expiration date hasn't passed.


You can search for users on the Customers page and view information in the Personal Area page.


Enjoy it!
